We are SATARA based School focused on excellence.

Initially Pre-primary & Primary(2009) followed by Secondary education(2017) started in the School.
Now this year(2018-2019) our school has succeeded to provide CBSE pattern education in our school.
Our motto is to stands for academic excellence, development of skill & character formation.

Why Choose Blue Diamond School

A community of lifelong learners, responsible global citizens, and champions of our own success.

Pre-primary Section – CBSE

At the Pre-primary level, teachers lay a lot of emphasis on the Play-Way method. Games are a natural means of teaching little ones and are the integral part of every classroom. Teachers extensively use picture reading to help the students grasp new concepts.

Primary Section- CBSE

In our primary section ( I to V standard–CBSE Pattern), we teach the children conversational skills, reading and writing and mental math. The students are groomed to hone their reading and listening skills for better learning of languages.

Secondary Section

Teachers use various techniques to inculcate the discipline of learning in them. They encourage students to perform role plays, make projects, grasp the practical knowledge of the subject promote creativity through prose and poetry pieces created by the students.

Excellent Infrastructure, ISO Certification

Our school has three storey excellent infrastructure with all the modern amenities. Separate designated areas as per the required sections.
Blue Diamond Eng. Med. School is the only school in lonand which possesses the ISO Certification

Qualified & Experience Faculty, E- Learning

Our school has best qualified, most experienced faculties & other staff.
E-Learning classroom makes study easier & improves students learning capabilities.
   

Science Laboratory, Library, Computer Lab

The School has well equipped laboratories For Physics, Chemistry and Biology. The School has a computer laboratories with 15 computers which are accessible to students. The school is equipped with a dedicated broadband Internet connection.
